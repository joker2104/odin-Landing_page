Destinations

Kanchenjunga

Located in the high-altitude region of India, Kanchenjunga National Park is a natural wonderland and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It hosts a rich diversity of wildlife, dazzling glaciers, tranquil lakes, and majestic mountains, among which rises the towering Mt. Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world.

Kasol

Kasol and Manali collectively form an ideal destination for enthusiasts of nature and adventure. Meanwhile, Manali, a popular hill station in the Kullu Valley, presents a plethora of activities such as skiing, paragliding, rafting, and camping for those seeking thrilling experiences in the lap of nature. Some trekking opportunities are:

  • Kheerganga
  • Tosh
  • Malana
  • Sar Pass

Rohtang La

Rohtang Pass is a gateway to a world of wonder and adventure. It is perched at an elevation of 3,978 metres (13,050 feet) on the Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas, bridging the verdant Kullu Valley with the stark Lahaul and Spiti Valleys. Rohtang Pass offers mesmerizing views of the snow-laden mountains, glaciers, rivers, and valleys. It also offers a plethora of exhilarating activities, such as skiing, snow scooter rides, paragliding, and mountain biking.

Shillong

Shillong, Tripura is a nature lover’s and adventure seeker’s paradise. It has various trekking trails that showcase the scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and wildlife diversity of the region. Some of the popular treks are:

  • David Scott Trail
  • Krem Mawsmai, Krem Mawmluh, and Krem Umshyrpi Caves Trail
  • Living Root Bridge Trail
  • Umden Village Trek
